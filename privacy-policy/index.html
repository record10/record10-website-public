Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3/6/2026

At Record10, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and software products (such as Record Flow and Record Bill).

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with our services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with our applications to improve our products.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information such as your name, email address, and the contents of the message you send us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our applications and website
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services
  • Understand and analyze how you use our products
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • Communicate with you for customer service or updates

3. Data Security

Record10 products operate primarily completely offline on your local machine. Your data (like recording logs, billing data) never leaves your device unless you explicitly configure a sync or backup mechanism. We do not have access to your locally stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Our website or apps may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
